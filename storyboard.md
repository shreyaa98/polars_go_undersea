Ilmar stepped into the ships storeroom. "I need some size 15 screws, they should be here". A shelf with open and half-open cargo boxes stood in the middle of the room as if it had been left there in a hurry. More cargo boxes were not very neatly piled up along the walls. When Ilmar stepped towards the shelf, he suddenly tripped over another box. He toppled over, rolled through the room and crashed into the shelf. The room collapsed in an avalanche of boxes and their content: tools, screws, rubber joins, fish tins and lots of other stuff.

When the dust settled, Ilmar raised his head from the debris. "Holy Polar," he muttered, "I guess I will have to clean up here."
